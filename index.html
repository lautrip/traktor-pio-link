Dual SysEx

Simultaneous ID 17 and ID 18 updates for maximum compatibility with DJM-S7, S9, and S11 mixers.

Auto-Scan

Plug and play architecture. Automatically detects Pioneer MIDI ports with ultra-low latency response.

QML Modding

One-click solution to export and install high-precision BPM tracking for Traktor Kontrol D2 and others.
